Design a professional mobile UI for a Tanzanian real estate marketplace app called **IPO**.

IPO connects property owners, madalali (agents), and property seekers. Users can browse, search, and contact agents to buy, rent, or book properties such as houses, apartments, land, villas, lodges, and short-stay properties.

The design should feel like a **serious property marketplace**, not a travel or booking platform.

The UI must be structured, clean, and realistic — similar in quality to apps like Zillow, Property24, and Airbnb — but localized for Tanzania.

Avoid generic AI-style layouts. Use proper visual hierarchy, spacing, and component structure.
